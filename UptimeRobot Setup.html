🏁 Keep RaceFusion Awake

UptimeRobot setup — free, takes 5 minutes
1
Create a free account

Go to uptimerobot.com and sign up for a free account. No credit card needed.

2
Add a new monitor

After logging in, click + Add New Monitor in the top left.

3
Configure the monitor

Set these values:

• Monitor Type: HTTP(s)

• Friendly Name: RaceFusion

• URL: your Streamlit app URL

https://your-app-name.streamlit.app

• Monitoring Interval: Every 5 minutes

4
Save the monitor

Click Create Monitor. UptimeRobot will immediately start pinging your app every 5 minutes, keeping it from sleeping.

5
Confirm it's working

Within a few minutes the monitor will show a green UP status. That means it's pinging successfully and your app will stay awake.

💡 Tip: UptimeRobot will also email you if your app ever goes down unexpectedly — useful to know when you have real users.
